Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14/11/2024

1. Introduction

At PontoHub, we value transparency and your privacy. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how and why we use them on our website (pontohub.com), and the options you have to manage your preferences. Our goal is to provide efficient, secure and personalised browsing, with continuous improvements to your experience on our website.

If you have any questions about the use of cookies on our website, please contact us at info@pontohub.com.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They have various functions, such as remembering your preferences and information, analysing traffic and improving website functionality. Cookies can be temporary (session only) or persistent (stored for a period).

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve website functionality, personalise your browsing and provide a more efficient experience. Our main objectives when using cookies are:

  • Ensure website security and performance: Essential cookies for basic and secure operation.
  • Analysis and improvement: Collect anonymous information to understand how visitors interact with the website and make adjustments to optimise your experience.
  • Personalisation: Save preferences such as language and other settings to adapt the website to your profile.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

To facilitate navigation, the cookies we use can be classified as:

  • Essential: Necessary for the basic functioning of the website, such as accessibility to secure areas. Without these cookies, some functions will not be available.
  • Performance: Collect anonymous data about how the website is used, helping to improve structure and content. These cookies do not identify the user individually.
  • Functional: Remember preferences and settings, such as language or region, to make your experience more personalised.
  • Marketing and Advertising: Record your visits and interactions to show relevant content and evaluate the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. This type of cookie may be shared with advertising partners.

5. Cookie Management

You can control the use of cookies directly in your browser, enabling, disabling or removing them as desired. Below, we indicate the basic procedure for some browsers:

  • Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Content Settings.
  • Firefox: Go to Preferences > Privacy and Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy.

Please note that disabling cookies may impact website functionality and limit some features.

6. Impact of Blocking Cookies

Blocking cookies may affect the user experience, preventing the proper functioning of some areas or features of the website, such as logging into restricted areas or retaining language preferences.

7. Changes and Updates

PontoHub may revise this Cookie Policy periodically to meet new regulations or operational needs. We recommend that you check this page regularly to stay updated.
